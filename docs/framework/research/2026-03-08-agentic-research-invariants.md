# Agentic Research Invariants

**Purpose:** Preserve the most stable research-backed truths that should influence almost every future design decision in this bundle.

---

## Invariants

1. `Consensus != proof.`
2. `Role != permission != approval authority.`
3. `More agents != always better.`
4. `Single-agent remains lawful for some bounded work.`
5. `Task shape must influence orchestration choice.`
6. `Artifact-driven handoff is safer than transcript dependence.`
7. `Source-sensitive work requires refresh against live guidance.`
8. `Security must be explicit and mapped, not implied by general review.`
9. `Verification burden must be allowed to rise independently of implementation complexity.`
10. `Stable terminology is required for a multi-document orchestration system.`
11. `Traceable route rationale is part of trustworthiness, not just observability polish.`
12. `Known unknowns should stay visible instead of being silently guessed into policy.`

---

## Use Rule

Treat these as `default design laws` unless later source refresh materially invalidates one and the invalidation is recorded.
